Disclaimer

The purpose of this web site is to explore various types of typographic and layout style for museum pages, as an end-of-course project for the "Information Modeling and Web Technologies" course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, under prof. Fabio Vitali.

The documents, images, and artworks contained in this web site have been selected for their historical and art-historical significance, as representative examples of Austrian painting and sculpture from the 18th to early 20th century. Their publication here is not intended to be an alternative to or replace their original locations. The original sources are listed below.

Image & Artwork Sources

All artworks are part of the permanent collection of the Belvedere Museum, Vienna, or are reproduced from open-access digital collections. Original sources:
